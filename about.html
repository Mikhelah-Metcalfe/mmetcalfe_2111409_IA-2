Celebrating Melanin-Rich Beauty

Mela-Plus is a beauty distribution brand dedicated to making high-quality, inclusive makeup easily accessible to Black and melanin-rich consumers. Built on the belief that beauty should reflect everyone, Mela-Plus curates and supplies products specifically designed to complement deeper skin tones, diverse undertones, and unique skin needs. The brand exists to bridge the gap between global beauty innovation and communities that have historically been underserved.

Through its platform, Mela-Plus brings together some of the most influential and inclusive brands in the industry, including Fenty Beauty, Juvia's Place, Pat McGrath Labs, Haus Labs, and NARS Cosmetics. By offering a carefully selected range of trusted products, Mela-Plus ensures customers have access to makeup that delivers both performance and representation.
